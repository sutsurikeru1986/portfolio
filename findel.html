Findel.co.uk was originally running on IBM Commerce and was sharing resource with another ecommerce website. It was being plagued with stability issues. I put forward to the business the option to migrate to WordPress which would save roughly £10,000 on support costs and over-time call outs per year. The primary focus of Findel plc's corporate website is store finanical information for potential investors and shareholders. WordPress was the ideal choice for keeping all the content up to date.
