Another large project working at Kleeneze was to reduce the time taken to complete and make the User Experience a more fluid experience for our dated distributor registration. The current process was very long and this caused a large number of drop outs from potential prospects part way though. I managed to reduce the process to 5 simple steps whilst only taking roughly 5 minutes to complete.

Made with using

Photoshop

Fireworks

Bootstrap

Wireframing

Kentico

More previews

The new distributor registration was released on April 1st 2014 (not an April fools!) and applicant numbers have already increased. We also got a lot of great feedback from our Distributor network as they didn't have to spend countless hours walking the prospect through the process of joining.
