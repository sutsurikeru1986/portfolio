As a long time fan of League of Legends and a avid streamer, I started to work with Riot Games's API to create a stats based app based on my summoner profile. Showcasing what league I was in with real time numbers for all my kills and deaths. It also gives a snapshot of all the champions I have played by featuring the number of times I have won or lost on a champion.

Made with using

Photoshop

Fireworks

Html/5

SASS

CSS3

PHP

For the future

This project is still in development, but lots of cool features are planned as Riot's API is full of stats I have yet to make use of! Below is what I have planned:

  • Unlimited match history (currently capped to 10 matches)
  • In-game stats such as final build, damage dealt and gold earned
  • Graphs, lots of graphs
  • Twitch.tv chat (Toggled)
